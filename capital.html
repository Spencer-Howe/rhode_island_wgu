Providence

Providence is the heart of Rhode Island, known for its vibrant culture, historical significance, and educational institutions.

  • Population: 179,883
  • Founded: 1636
  • Region: New England
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $42,158

Interesting Facts

  • Home to Brown University, an Ivy League institution located in Providence.
  • Known for WaterFire, a public art installation and festival held in Providence.
  • One of the oldest cities in the United States, Providence was founded in 1636.

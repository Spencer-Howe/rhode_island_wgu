Rhode Island

About Rhode Island

Rhode Island, known as "The Ocean State," is celebrated for its stunning coastal towns, rich colonial history, and vibrant cultural scenes.

  • Population: 1,097,379
  • Incorporated: 1790
  • Region: New England
  • Classification: Mix of urban, suburban, and rural
  • Average Income: $70,000

Interesting Facts

  • Home to the oldest synagogue in the United States, Touro Synagogue, located in Newport.
  • Famous for its sandy shores and seaside colonial towns, with over 400 miles of coastline.
  • The first state to declare independence from British rule on May 4, 1776.

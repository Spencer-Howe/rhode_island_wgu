Newport

About Newport

Newport is a seaside city on Aquidneck Island known for its historic mansions and rich maritime history.

  • Population: 24,779
  • Founded: 1639
  • Region: New England
  • Classification: Urban
  • Average Income: $68,250

Interesting Facts

  • Home to the oldest synagogue in the United States, Touro Synagogue.
  • Famous for its historic mansions, including The Breakers and Marble House.
  • Host of the annual Newport Jazz Festival and Newport Folk Festival.

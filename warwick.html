Warwick

About

Warwick is known for its beautiful coastlines and historical significance in Rhode Island's development.

  • Population: 81,004
  • Founded: 1642
  • Region: New England
  • Classification: Suburban
  • Average Income: $60,000

Interesting Facts

  • Home to the T.F. Green Airport, Rhode Island's main airport.
  • Known for its role in King Philip's War in the 17th century.
  • Features several historic villages, including Pawtuxet Village.
